Meet our Team

Bryan Henson, DVM

Practice Owner & CEO

I find veterinary medicine a very exciting and satisfying career and enjoy everyday I am blessed with the opportunity to assist families with their pet companions. I look forward to educational seminars and programs that allow me to utilize more effective prevention programs and sound treatments for sick animals. Communicating with and educating pet owners on the importance of preventive care when pets are healthy and treatment plans when their pet is sick or injured are ways I can share what I have learned.

Karla Valdez, DVM

Co-Founder & Veterinarian

My plans to become a veterinarian began at age 10 when pet sitting for neighbors and family. So, with determination and a love for animals, I graduated from UTA with a Biomedical Science degree in 1993 and a Doctorate in Veterinary Medicine in 1997. I have been practicing in the Dallas-Fort Worth area for over sixteen years. Areas of special interest are dermatology, internal medicine, and suturing skills in wound care. I truly enjoy educating new pet owners about their puppy, kitten, or adult dogs and cats and how, together, we can give their pet the best care for a happy life.

Isabel Cutillar, DVM

Veterinarian

I am so happy to be a member of the wonderful staff here at FCAH, supporting the doctors and team members as they care for our clients' animal companions! With very few exceptions, my life has always been shared with a dog, a cat, or one of a menagerie of small animal pets. My working background is comprised of 18 plus years of office management in specialty medical practices in Austin and Dallas, plus support staff positions in medical/dental since 1976. I am thankful everyday for knowledge I have gained thus far and the opportunity to work and learn among the caring staff in this well established, dedicated, and passionate veterinary practice.
